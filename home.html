Home Play


The game is typically played between two people, but can also be played in larger groups.

Gestures

Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand:
  • Rock (a fist)
  • Paper (an open hand)
  • Scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V)

    Draws:

    If both players choose the same gesture, the game is a draw, and typically played again.

    Gameplay :

    Players typically count to three in unison, then simultaneously display their chosen gesture. It's important that players reveal their gestures at the same time to ensure fairness.

    Best of Five :

    To determine a winner, multiple rounds are often played, with the first player to win a specified number of rounds declared the overall winner. The most common format is best of three, but you can choose any number of rounds.

    Fairness :

    To ensure fairness, players should keep their chosen gesture hidden until the moment of reveal, and should avoid any verbal or non-verbal cues that might give away their choice.
